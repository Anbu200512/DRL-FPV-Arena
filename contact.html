FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I register for an event?

Simply create an account, go to the Events page, select your desired event, and click "Register Now". You'll receive a confirmation email with details.

What equipment do I need to start FPV training?

For beginners, we recommend starting with our simulator training. For actual flight, you'll need an FPV drone, goggles, transmitter, batteries, and charger. Visit our shop for recommended starter bundles.

Do you offer refunds for training courses?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all training modules if you're not satisfied with your purchase.

How can I become a sponsor?

Please email sponsorship@drl-fpv.com with your company details and sponsorship inquiry, and our team will get back to you within 48 hours.
